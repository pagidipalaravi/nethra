What is forof loop?
	The javascript for of statement loops through the values of an iterable object.
	The for of will let you loop over iterable data structures such as : Arrays, Strings, Maps, NodeLists and more.
What is the advantage of using for of loop?
	The for of loop will make the better performance than regular for loop.
	And for of loop will avoid the mistakes which we may do in the regular for loop.
	And also it will execute fast when compared to regular for loop.
	syntax : 
		for(variable of iterable){
			//code block to be executed
		}
	variable - For every iteration the value of next property will assigned to the variable, variable can be declared with const, let or var.
	iterable - An object that has iterable properties.
