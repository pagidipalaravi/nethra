What is the use of break statement?
	The break statement jumps out of the loop.
	It will terminate the execution of current loop or condition and control will comes out of loop or condition.
	It will end the loop when using break statement.
What is the use of continue statement?
	The continue statement is used to break one iteration in the loop.
	It will break particular iteration, if a condition occurs and continues with the next iteration in the loop.
